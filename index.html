Spatialproteomics - an interoperable toolbox for analyzing highly multiplexed fluorescence images¶

Spatialproteomics is an interoperable toolbox for analyzing high-dimensional imaging datasets. Built on top of xarray and dask, spatialproteomics can process images that are larger than the working memory. It supports synchronization of shared coordinates across data modalities such as images, segmentation masks, and expression matrices, which facilitates easy and safe subsetting and transformation.
